About This App

Welcome to our retro-themed pomodoro app! This project is designed to bring you back to the nostalgic era of 8-bit and 9-bit gaming. Whether you're here to use the timer or just explore, we hope you enjoy the pixel-perfect aesthetics and the classic vibes.

Built with love for all things retro, this app combines the simplicity of old-school design with modern functionality. Enjoy your stay, and feel free to reach out with any feedback!

- The Dev Team
